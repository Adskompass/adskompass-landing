Frequently asked questions

How does the AI work?+

Our AI continuously reviews your campaign performance — spend, audience response, creative fatigue — and generates specific, explained recommendations. It never acts on its own; every change is sent to you first for approval, typically via Telegram.

Is my Meta account safe?+

Yes. Access credentials are encrypted at rest, and built-in safety controls — daily spend caps, budget change limits, campaign-age locks — prevent any single action from going too far, even an approved one.

Is my data safe?+

We handle your data in line with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). Personal data is deleted within 30 days of account cancellation; we don't sell or share your data with third parties.

What if I'm not satisfied?+

Every plan includes a first-month money-back guarantee, covering your subscription fee and valid until you've approved 3 recommendations on your account. There's no lock-in contract — you can cancel anytime after that.

Can I try before committing?+

Yes — request a free audit report first. Grant read-only access to your Meta account and we'll send you a full analysis of where your campaigns stand today, no commitment required.

How is this different from an agency?+

Agencies typically charge RM 3,000–8,000/month and often lock you into long contracts. AdsKompass starts from RM 444/month, monitors your account every day instead of a periodic check-in, and never makes a change without your explicit approval.

Do I need technical knowledge?+

No. Recommendations are written in plain language, and you approve or reject them with a single tap on Telegram — no Meta Ads Manager expertise required.
